History snapshots & replay

You can now scrub through a run's full history — every task, every signal, every diff. Replay from any snapshot to debug failing pipelines without re-running everything upstream.

Added

  • History panel with snapshot scrubbing and per-task diff view#412
  • Replay from snapshot — restart a pipeline at any prior state#428
  • New --dry-run flag for tagma run#431

Changed

  • Wire routing for lane-crossing edges now uses a short entry stub#440
  • Task card width adapts to canvas width between 158–198px

Fixed

  • Connection lines were offset by the lane rail width in rare DPI cases#436
  • OpenCode driver hanging on SIGPIPE#422
